Breakazine

How to rest in peace-less cities

‘Breakazine’ is a bimonthly thematic publication which has the depth of a book and the breadth of magazine. It places emphasis on the ‘integration of concepts’ and ‘vital interests’ in order to trigger teenagers’ curiosity and improve their self-learning ability through the daily life examples in the magazine.
